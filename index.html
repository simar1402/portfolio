I am currently working as a Product Designer at Internshala. I am mostly working on the Internshala Student Partner side, my work majorly involves making intuitive designs, making user experience better, create catchy UIs which attracts our audience.

I worked as a UI/UX Designer at Minimal Duo, a product design service company wherein I worked on several mobile applications such as Calm Sleep, Go Fit, Unlu etc. It was a massive learning experience, followed end to end process for designing the apps.

I worked as a Graphic Designer and Social Media Manager in the company. I majorly worked on Adobe Illustrator CC 2019 and Adobe Photoshop 2019. My major responisibilties were to handle client related work such as Logo designing, pitch designing and creating social media creatives for the company.
